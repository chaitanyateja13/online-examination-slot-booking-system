# User Guide

## Introduction

The Online Examination Slot Booking System provides a simple interface for students and faculty to manage examination-related activities.

---

# Student Workflow

## Step 1: Login

Students log in using their registered credentials.

---

## Step 2: Dashboard

After login, students can:

- View profile
- View available examination slots
- Book examination slots
- Download hall tickets
- Make payments
- View examination results
- Receive notifications

---

## Step 3: Book Examination Slot

Students can:

- Select an examination
- Choose an available slot
- Confirm booking

---

## Step 4: Payment

Students complete payment before the examination.

---

## Step 5: Download Hall Ticket

After successful booking and payment, students can download their hall ticket.

---

## Step 6: View Results

Students can access examination results after publication.

---

# Faculty Workflow

Faculty members can:

- Login
- Manage examination schedules
- Monitor slot bookings
- Publish examination results
- Send notifications

---

# Security

The application provides:

- Authentication
- Authorization
- Secure session management
- CSRF protection

---

# Logout

Users should always log out after completing their session.
